ABOUT COMPANY

Excavon Supplies Ltd is a trusted supplier of high-quality Personal Protective Equipment (PPE), Mechanical Engineering Spares, Electrical Spares, Laboratory Items & Reagents, and Instrumentation Spares for the mining industry. Our comprehensive product range and industry expertise ensure that mining operations run efficiently, safely, and without disruption.


With a commitment to excellence, we partner with top manufacturers and suppliers to provide reliable, durable, and cost-effective solutions tailored to the demanding conditions of mining environments. Headquartered in South Africa, we proudly serve mining operations across the Sub-Saharan region, ensuring timely delivery and localized support to meet industry needs.


Our dedicated team brings decades of combined experience in the mining sector, allowing us to understand the unique challenges and requirements of our clients. We've built our reputation on providing not just products, but complete solutions that address the complex needs of modern mining operations.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to be the leading provider of essential mining supplies and engineering solutions in South Africa and the sub-Saharan region. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of safety and efficiency, empowering our clients to achieve operational excellence in their mining operations.


To drive innovation and reliability in mining supply chain solutions, enhancing productivity and sustainability across the industry. We aim to establish long-term partnerships with our clients by consistently delivering exceptional value through our products and services.


We are dedicated to understanding each client's unique challenges and providing customized solutions that address their specific operational needs, helping them optimize their processes and achieve sustainable growth.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to transform mining supply chains across Sub-Saharan Africa by introducing innovative, sustainable, and efficient solutions that elevate operational standards across the industry. We aspire to be the catalyst for technological advancement in mining operations.
